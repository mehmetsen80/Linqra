Provider agrees to perform the services described in one or more Statements of Work ("SOW") signed by the parties (the "Services").

**2.1 Fees.** Client shall pay Provider the fees set forth in the applicable SOW.

## 3. TERM AND TERMINATION
**3.1 Term.** This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and shall continue for a period of five (5) years ("Initial Term").
**3.2 Automatic Renewal.** This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive five (5) year periods unless Provider gives Client written notice of non-renewal at least 180 days prior to the end of the then-current term. Client has no right to prevent renewal.
**3.3 Termination.** Provider may terminate this Agreement at any time for convenience upon ten (10) days' written notice. Client may only terminate this Agreement for material breach by Provider that remains uncured for ninety (90) days after written notice.
